# JNTUH Connect Privacy Policy

Effective: July 14, 2026

This policy explains the data handled by the JNTUH Connect iOS app and backend.

## Data you provide

- **Hall ticket numbers:** When you request a result, backlog report, credits check, comparison, or class report, the app sends the entered hall ticket number to the JNTUH Connect backend. It is used only to provide and secure the requested service.

The app does not require an account, advertising identifier, precise location, contacts, microphone, camera, or photo-library access. It does not upload documents, photos, or videos.

## Data stored on your device

- Up to eight recent student summaries containing name, hall ticket number, and branch.
- Up to two recently opened document shortcuts, which expire after 24 hours.
- Your appearance preference.

Full result responses are not persisted by the iOS app. You can remove recent student summaries and document shortcuts from **Settings > Data > Clear saved data**. Removing the app also removes its local data.

## Backend processing and retention

The backend processes result requests and may cache university result data so it can provide the requested features. Backend records are retained only as long as needed to operate and secure the service, resolve related support, and meet applicable legal obligations.

To request deletion of backend data, open an [App Support request](https://github.com/ThilakReddyy/jntuhconnect-ios/issues) asking the maintainer to arrange a private contact channel. **Do not post a hall ticket number, marksheet, or other personal data in a public issue.** Identity or authorization may need to be verified before data relating to a student is disclosed or deleted.

## Sharing and tracking

JNTUH Connect does not use third-party advertising or analytics SDKs, sell personal data, or track people across apps and websites. Data is shared only with service infrastructure needed to provide the requested JNTUH Connect feature, or when required by law. Service providers are expected to protect data consistently with this policy.

The app opens selected university documents, official result pages, and community links in a visible Safari view. Those external services have their own privacy practices. API requests use HTTPS. Some official JNTUH result links may be served by the university over HTTP; do not enter credentials or other information on those pages.

## Your choices

You can choose not to submit a result request. You can clear data stored by the app at any time and may request deletion of data retained by the backend using the process above.

## Changes

Material changes to this policy will be published in this file and reflected in the policy date. App Store privacy disclosures will be updated when data practices change.

## Contact

For privacy, safety, or support questions, use [JNTUH Connect App Support](https://github.com/ThilakReddyy/jntuhconnect-ios/issues). Do not include personal academic data in a public issue.
